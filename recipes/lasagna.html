Lasagna

Lasagna is a dish loved by people and fat orange cats alike. Originating in Italy, it was popularized throughout the world in the 23rd century

Ingredients

  • Big flat noodle
  • Red sauce
  • Crumbly cheese
  • meat
  1. Spread meat and sauce in to a pan
  2. Add a layer of flat noodle
  3. Add cheese
  4. Repeat 1000x
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 500 K for six days
  6. consume
